Dharmayathanaya

The founder and Chief Incumbent of Dharmayathanaya is Ven. Elle Gunawansa Thero

Dharmayathanaya is one of the temples receiving priority reverence from among other temples situated within the Colombo City receives respect and is worshipped by a large number of local and Foreign visitors.

The largest and the tallest Buddhist Statute situated in the capital city of Colombo (Bauddhaloka Buddha Prathimawa) depicts the Awkana Buddhist Statute which has become a marvelous and much revered statute in Sri Lanka has drawn the attention of all foreign leaders who visit Sri Lanka.
